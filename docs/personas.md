# SmartDrobe User Personas

## Overview
Understanding the target audience is crucial for SmartDrobe's product development. Our primary users are professionals and fashion enthusiasts who experience decision fatigue or struggle with wardrobe organization.

---

## 1. The Busy Professional (Primary Persona)
**Name:** Emily Chen  
**Age:** 28  
**Occupation:** Marketing Manager  
**Location:** Urban City Center

### Background
Emily has a demanding job and a busy social life. She owns a lot of clothes but tends to wear the same 5 outfits because she doesn't have the time or energy to plan combinations in the morning.

### Pain Points
* **Decision Fatigue:** Wastes 15+ minutes every morning staring at her closet.
* **Underutilization:** Forgets about clothes she owns because they are buried in the back of her wardrobe.
* **Context Mismatch:** Sometimes dresses inappropriately for the weather or the specific office-to-dinner transition because she rushed.

### User Motivations
* **Efficiency:** Wants to look put-together with zero morning effort.
* **Confidence:** Desires the feeling of being well-dressed for any occasion.
* **Return on Investment:** Wants to actually wear the clothes she spent money on.

### How SmartDrobe Helps
* **Daily Generator:** Provides instant, weather-aware outfit recommendations.
* **Calendar Planning:** Allows her to schedule her outfits on Sunday for the entire workweek.

## 2. The Sustainable Organizer (Secondary Persona)
**Name:** Marcus Johnson  
**Age:** 32  
**Occupation:** Software Developer  
**Location:** Tech Hub City

### Background
Marcus cares about sustainability and prefers a minimalist approach to his wardrobe. He wants to know exactly what he owns and get the most wear out of every single item.

### Pain Points
* **Overconsumption:** Wants to avoid buying redundant items simply because he forgot he already owned something similar.
* **Tracking:** Has no data on which items he actually wears versus which ones just take up space.

### User Motivations
* **Organization:** Loves cataloging and having a digital twin of his physical space.
* **Sustainability:** Wants to lower his fashion carbon footprint by maximizing item wear.

### How SmartDrobe Helps
* **Virtual Closet:** Provides a clean, filterable digital inventory of all his items.
* **Analytics/Sustainability Tracker:** Shows him his most-worn items and calculates the carbon footprint of his wardrobe.
